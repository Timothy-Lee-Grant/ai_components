# Northwind Robotics — Employee Handbook (Excerpt)

## Working hours and remote work
Our standard work week is 40 hours. Core collaboration hours are 10am to 4pm in
your local time zone. Employees may work remotely up to three days per week.
Fully remote arrangements require manager and HR approval and are reviewed yearly.

## Paid time off
Full-time employees accrue 20 days of paid vacation per year, plus 10 company
holidays. Unused vacation up to 5 days may be carried into the next calendar year.
Sick leave is separate and uncapped, taken in good faith.

## Equipment and expenses
Each employee receives a laptop and a $500 home-office stipend in their first month.
Work-related expenses under $100 may be approved by your manager; anything above
$100 requires a purchase order filed through the Finance portal.

## Security policy
All laptops must use full-disk encryption and the company password manager.
Two-factor authentication is mandatory for email, code repositories, and the VPN.
Report any lost device to security@northwind.example within 24 hours.

## Parental leave
New parents receive 16 weeks of fully paid leave, which can be taken any time
within the first 12 months after the birth or adoption of a child.

## Code review
Every change to production code requires at least one approving review. Changes to
the billing or authentication services require two approvals, one of which must be
from a senior engineer.

# AquaBot 3000 — Product FAQ

## What is the AquaBot 3000?
The AquaBot 3000 is an automatic plant-watering robot for homes and small offices.
It monitors soil moisture and waters up to 12 plants on a schedule you set in the app.

## What is the water tank capacity?
The onboard tank holds 4 liters, enough for roughly two weeks of watering for an
average set of 6 houseplants before it needs a refill.

## Is it battery powered?
The AquaBot 3000 runs on a rechargeable battery that lasts about 30 days per charge.
It charges via USB-C and shows battery status in the companion app.

## What connectivity does it use?
It connects over 2.4GHz Wi-Fi. Bluetooth is used only for the initial setup pairing.
There is no support for 5GHz Wi-Fi at this time.

## What is the warranty?
The AquaBot 3000 comes with a 2-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects.
Water damage caused by user misuse, such as overfilling the tank, is not covered.

## How much does it cost?
The AquaBot 3000 retails for $149. A premium subscription, AquaBot Plus, costs
$5 per month and adds plant-specific watering profiles and seasonal adjustments.
